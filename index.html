About Me


Hello there! I'm Fatima, a junior developer on a thrilling journey through the realm of code. My toolbox includes Python, C#, ASP.NET, SQL, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - a mix I wield with enthusiasm and a dash of creativity.
